About

Character-driven.
Built to perform.

I'm Beckham, a 19-year-old voice actor, content creator, and director. I specialize in animation, stop-motion, and video game voice work, with a natural midrange voice that goes deep and handles accents with ease.

Since early 2026, I've been deep in the work, booked roles across indie animated series, brickfilms, and a Steam game, with more always in the pipeline. I take direction well, deliver fast, and bring no ego to the booth.

Beyond voice acting, I'm building my own animated series (Mono No Aware), learning 2D and 3D animation, and working toward the long game: owning my own studio and creating original shows.

"Comfortable taking direction and delivering fast turnarounds. Not interested in audiobooks or comic dubbing, animation, games, and stop-motion are the focus."
